The real reason marks slip

You know the material. The format is what surprises you.

Slides and PDFs teach content — but the first time most students meet the actual question mix, timer and flow is during the graded exam. Canv.io removes that surprise by letting you rehearse the real thing, as many times as you want.

Features

Everything you need to rehearse — nothing you don't.

A focused practice gym, not another note-taking app.

Regenerating exams

Every attempt draws a fresh paper from a question bank. The same mock exam becomes an endless supply of past papers.

Familiar exam interface

A clean LMS-style quiz: question navigator, live timer, autosave and submit. The environment matches what you'll actually sit.

Instant, explained grading

Multiple-choice, fill-in-the-blank and matching grade automatically — with the correct answer and a short explanation on every question.

Progress & attempt history

Scores, a pass target and a saved history of every attempt — review any past paper to see exactly what you got wrong.

Topic-focused practice

Drill only the parts you're weak on — run exams scoped to a single topic, or the full real-format paper.

Private & offline

No account needed to start. Everything runs in your browser and your attempts stay on your device.
